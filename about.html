Who We Are

The Vintage Realtors brings decades of combined experience in Kenyan property markets. We combine thoughtful listing presentation, strategic marketing, and local market intelligence to help sellers and buyers make better property decisions.

Our services span property listings, professional photography and virtual tours, valuation advice, and advisory services for developers and investors. We take pride in curated, honest listings that connect the right buyers and tenants with the right properties.

How We Work

We begin with a careful assessment of the property, followed by tailored marketing packages that include photography, floor plans, and targeted online exposure. Every listing receives individual attention and performance tracking.
